# Agentic AI — Quiz Questions

---

### Question 1
**What does "Agentic AI" mean?**

A) AI that only answers questions when asked
B) AI that can independently make decisions and take actions to achieve goals
C) AI that replaces all human workers
D) AI that only works offline

**Answer: B**

---

### Question 2
**What is the "brain" of a modern AI agent?**

A) A calculator
B) A search engine
C) A Large Language Model (LLM)
D) A database

**Answer: C**

---

### Question 3
**What does the ReAct pattern stand for?**

A) Read and Edit
B) Reasoning and Acting
C) Record and Activate
D) Receive and Transmit

**Answer: B**

---

### Question 4
**Which of these is an example of a "tool" an AI agent can use?**

A) Web search
B) A keyboard
C) A monitor
D) A mouse

**Answer: A**

---

### Question 5
**What is the main difference between a chatbot and an AI agent?**

A) A chatbot is faster
B) An AI agent can plan, use tools, and take actions on its own
C) A chatbot is more intelligent
